Privacy policy

Last updated: 01.03.2025

Welcome to Light Up Design. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

  1. Information we collect

    We collect and process limited information to ensure the functionality of our website. At this stage, we do not use cookies for marketing or analytics purposes. The types of cookies we use include:

    • Strictly necessary cookies — These cookies are essential for the basic functionality of our website, such as security, accessibility, and navigation. They do not store any personally identifiable information.
    • Marketing cookies — These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns. You can opt in or out of these cookies at any time through our cookie management settings.
    • Personalization cookies — These cookies help us remember your preferences, such as language selection, to improve your browsing experience.
    • Analytics cookies — These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website, allowing us to improve performance and functionality.

  2. Legal basis for processing data

    We process personal data based on the following legal grounds under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

    • Legitimate interest: For ensuring website functionality and security.
    • Consent: For the use of marketing, personalization, and analytics cookies. You can withdraw consent at any time.

  3. Your rights under GDPR

    As a user within the European Union, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

    • Right to access: You may request information about the personal data we hold about you.
    • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct or update inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
    • Right to erasure: You may request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
    • Right to restrict processing: You have the right to limit how we process your personal data.
    • Right to object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
    • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a structured format for transfer to another service.
    • Right to withdraw consent: If we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
    • Right to lodge a complaint: If you believe we are violating GDPR, you have the right to file a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

  4. Data retention policy

    We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy. If required by law, we may retain certain data for compliance obligations.

  5. International data transfers

    If we store or process data outside the EU/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other legal mechanisms.

  6. Data sharing and third-party services

    At this time, we do not share your information with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes. If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws.

  7. Your rights and choices

    You have control over your cookie preferences. You can adjust settings within your browser or through our website’s cookie management tool to accept or reject certain cookies.

  8. Updates to this privacy policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with the updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

  9. Contact us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, you may contact us at:

    Light Up Design

    hello@lightupdesign.co

    By continuing to use our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

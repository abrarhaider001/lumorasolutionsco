Projects that illuminate brands

Discover how our strategic design transforms ideas into impact — explore a curated showcase of work that blends creativity, clarity, and measurable results.

1. GreenLeaf Living – Sustainable Interior Design Website

Web Design
Sustainability Brand
Responsive UI/UX

A modern, eco-conscious website for GreenLeaf Living, showcasing handcrafted furniture and decor made from renewable materials. The site blends elegant design with educational storytelling, a carbon impact calculator, and an engaging shopping experience — all focused on reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainable living.

2. Golden Crust Bakery – Artisan Bakery Website Design

Web Design
Food &Beverage Brand
Mobile-First UI

A warm and inviting website for Golden Crust Bakery, designed to showcase fresh-baked goods and the brand’s rich heritage. The site combines clean layout, enticing product photography, and a mobile-first approach to create a seamless ordering experience. With curated sections for packages, top sellers, and seasonal deals, the website strengthens brand trust while driving local sales and customer engagement.

3. WeeClean – Cleaning Service Booking Website

Web Design
Service Platform
Cleaning Service

A clean and conversion-focused website for WeeClean, a professional cleaning company offering home and office services. The design prioritizes clarity and trust, with step-by-step booking, service highlights, and mobile -friendly layouts. With intuitive navigation and bold CTAs, the site helps users quickly book services, compare plans, and feel confident in the brand 's reliability.

4. Pantone Art Fair –
Event Website Design

Web Design
Event Website
Dark Theme Design

A bold and immersive website for Pantone Art Fair — an event celebrating local art, creativity, and community. This concept-focused design uses a vibrant dark mode, animated elements, and dynamic layout to attract visitors and boost ticket engagement. Designed with responsiveness and performance in mind, the site works seamlessly across devices and invites users to explore schedules, artists, and event details with ease.

5. Photography Masterclass – Online Course Platform

E-Learning Website
Dark UI Design
Responsive Layout

A sleek, conversion-optimized website for an online photography masterclass. Built with a modern dark interface, the site delivers video-based lessons, certification badges, and engaging course navigation. The platform is fully responsive and designed to provide a seamless experience for learners on any device — ideal for creators, hobbyists, and aspiring professionals looking to sharpen their skills through intuitive learning paths.

6. DesignCo – Digital Agency Website

Web Design for Agencies
Responsive UI Showcase
Conversion-Focused Landing Page

A bold and modern website concept for DesignCo, a digital agency focused on creating user-first, result-driven websites. This project features a strong typographic hierarchy, vibrant call-to-action buttons, and dynamic layout to capture attention and drive conversions. Designed with a mobile-first approach and seamless responsiveness across all breakpoints, the interface highlights the agency’s portfolio, services, and brand personality with clarity and impact. Ideal for agencies looking to scale client acquisition through compelling design and strategy.

FAQs

Got Questions? We 've Got Answers!

How do I get started working with Light Up Design?

It’s simple! Fill out the contact form, and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours to schedule an initial consultation. From there, we’ll discuss your needs, goals, and how we can help.

What services do you offer?

We specialize in UX/UI design, design strategy, and digital brand audit. Additionally, we provide  Webflow implementation, redesigns, and complete end-to-end design solutions.

What industries do you work with?

Our expertise spans a variety of industries, including tech startups, e-commerce, education, and B2B services. We adapt our approach to fit the unique needs of each client.

What’s your design process?

Our process involves:

  • Discovery: Understanding your goals and challenges.
  • Strategy &Planning: Creating a roadmap tailored to your objectives.
  • Design &Prototyping: Crafting user-focused designs and prototypes.
  • Execution: Delivering polished and functional solutions. We ensure clear communication at every step.
How long does it take to complete a project?

Timelines vary depending on the project scope. A small project may take 2–4 weeks, while more complex solutions could take several months. We’ll provide a clear timeline after discussing your requirements.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes! Our team is globally distributed, and we’re experienced in working with clients worldwide. No matter your location, we can collaborate seamlessly.

What’s your pricing structure?

Our pricing is tailored to each project based on its complexity, scope, and requirements. After the initial consultation, we’ll provide a transparent, customized quote.

Do you offer ongoing support after project delivery?

Yes, we offer post-launch support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution remains effective and up-to-date. We’re here to assist even after the project is complete.

Can you help redesign my existing website?

Absolutely! We specialize in redesign projects, focusing on improving usability, aesthetics, and functionality to meet your current goals and user needs.

How do you ensure the project aligns with my brand?

We begin with an in-depth discovery phase, where we analyze your brand, audience, and goals. Throughout the process, we maintain open communication to ensure the final design reflects your brand’s identity.
